# 📈SQL-Data-Analysis
============================================================================================
## 🔍Overview
Performed end-to-end data analysis on customer, product, and sales datasets from a data warehouse using SQL.
Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and applied advanced analytics techniques, including change over time,
cumulative analysis, performance analysis, proportional analysis, and data segmentation to generate actionable 
business insights.

## 📖Customer Report
The customer report is designed to consolidate key metrics and behavioral patterns. It captures essential details 
such as customer information, age, and transaction history, while segmenting customers into categories like VIP, 
Regular, and New, along with age groups.
       
It further evaluates customer performance by measuring total orders, sales, quantity purchased, number of
products, and customer lifespan. Key indicators such as recency, average order value, and average monthly spend are
calculated to better understand engagement and value.

## 📰Product Report
The product report focuses on evaluating product performance and contribution to revenue. It includes important
attributes like product name, category, subcategory, and cost, and classifies products into high, mid, and low performers 
based on revenue.
        
The analysis measures total orders, sales, quantity sold, and unique customers, along with product lifespan. It 
also derives key metrics such as recency, average order revenue, and average monthly revenue to assess product efficiency 
and demand.

## ⚒️Tools & Technologies
 * SQL (T-SQL)
 * SQL Server
 * Data warehouse structure (Bronze, Silver, Gold)
